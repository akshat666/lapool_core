HOP OR DROP - FAQs "Ride-sharing is simple as black and white."
HOP OR DROP is a simple, real-time yet powerful ride-sharing(carpooling) mobile application.
It connects you to your network of people when traveling to your destination.

When you launch the app, there are two options you can choose from:-
HOP - Request other users to pick you up for a ride to your destination
DROP - Pickup and drop a user who has requested for a ride

 We are a world apart from other apps, for instance :-
  • Simple and to the point. No complicated steps, no annoying forms.
  • Option to ride with only your own people from your social networks like Facebook, etc
  • Real time requests - Tap and get a ride
  • Safety features like tracking and SOS included
  • No sharing of mobile numbers or other personal details
  • Women can share with women travelers
    • There is more so kindly install the application and see it yourself.

         If you have a vehicle and you are willing to offer drops to other users, you can define your regular fixed "Paths". Example, your "Path" to work or College. Once you define your "Path", the application will optimize and match other users' HOP requests to your PATH. We do not charge our users. The app is free to use.
    We also encourage our users to offer rides for free or a very minimal amount which covers their fuel costs. This amount can be transacted in cash by the travelers.
    It is advisable to chat with the travelers (using our inbuilt chat feature) before you board the vehicle, and clarify if any amount will be charged(for splitting the fuel cost etc).

         Our goal is to provide the safest platform for ride-sharing for our users.
  • Women can ride with women travelers only
  • We provide "Tracking" feature where you can send your trip's changing location updates to your family or friends in real time.
  • We also have an emergency SOS feature which will send your location updates to your emergency contacts.
    • But the truth is nothing can be completely safe in this universe. Be smart and use your common sense when sharing a ride with anyone.
    We recommend you to read our terms including the section on safety.
