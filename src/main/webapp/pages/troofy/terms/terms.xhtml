  • There are safety features in the application like "Track Trip" or "SOS Alert" which you can track your location in case of emergency. But these options can be unreliable depending on the signal strength and other factors. We recommend calling the police in case of any emergency.

  • As a DROPPER use the chat feature in the application for clarifying any conditions with the HOPPER
  • Do not use mobile phone when driving or riding.
  • Any misbehaviour, or misconduct by the DROPPER or the HOPPER should be immediately reported to the police;
  • In case of rash or dangerous driving or riding, report the incident to the police
